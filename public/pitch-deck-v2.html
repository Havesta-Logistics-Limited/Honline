A density-first playbook
Phase 01 · Lagos

Validate the cold-chain corridor.

2026 · Pre-seed deployment
  • Deploy first cold-chain pilot unit in Lagos
  • Onboard 20–50 farmers across producing states
  • Sign 5–10 anchor B2B clients (restaurants, retailers)
  • Launch consumer app private beta
  • Validate unit economics and reduce spoilage 40%+

Scale beyond Lagos. Prove the playbook.

2026–2027 · Seed round
  • Expand to Abuja (and Kano if signal-strong)
  • Grow farmer network to 500+; B2B clients to 80+

  • Open marketplace API to third-party producers

2028+ · Series A
  • Expand into Ghana, Kenya, and West Africa cities
